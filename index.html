About Me

My name is Foivos and I come from the beatiful island of Corfu, Greece. During the last 6 years I live and work in Basel, Switzerland.

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the lab of Professor Helge Grosshans at FMI, Basel Switzerland. working on computational methods to study RNA function and processing. I am developing software and pipelines to analyze High Throuput Sequencing (HTS) data ranging from RNA-seq, poly(A)-seq and ribo-seq to single cells and third generation sequencing technologies (PacBio, Oxford Nanopore). At the same time, I am collaborating with experimental biologists to help them with the analysis and interpretation of their data.

I obtained a Ph.D. in Bioinformatics from the University of Basel, Switzerland (advisor: Professor Mihaela Zavolan). I also hold an MSc in Bioinformatics from the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, Greece (Department of Biology) and a diploma (5 year's course) in Electronics and Computer Engineering from the Techincal University of Crete, Greece.

You can find a list of publications on Google Scholar.
